About Us

About Us

Agromart is a key division of CEC, established with the vision of promoting high-quality local agricultural products to the market. The Agromart is owned and guided by Mr. Ashoka Randeniya, whose commitment to supporting local farmers and delivering nutritious, high-quality food products plays a vital role in the growth of the organization

We offer a wide range of locally sourced food and beverage products, carefully selected to ensure freshness, quality, and nutritional value. By working closely with local farmers and producers, Agromart supports sustainable agriculture while introducing innovative products suited to modern lifestyles.

Our mission is to provide safe, affordable, and healthy local products while strengthening the local economy and building long-term trust with our customers. At Agromart, we believe in promoting healthy living through locally grown goodness.


Chairman

A New Beginning — Rebranded from CEC Agromart

FreshCoMart is the newest retail and supermarket brand of the CEC Group, proudly reimagined to deliver a superior, modern shopping experience. With flagship outlets in Nawala, Colombo 07, and Kurunegala, FreshCoMart is set to expand islandwide within the next year.

Driven by the visionary leadership of Eng. Ashoka Randeni, Chairman & Managing Director of CEC Group, FreshCoMart was founded on a simple yet powerful concept: delivering the freshest vegetables and fruits directly from CEC’s own farmlands at the lowest possible price.

By integrating farm-to-store supply chains, modern agricultural practices, and efficient distribution, FreshCoMart ensures unmatched freshness while supporting Sri Lanka’s agricultural sector and sustainable development.

We guarantee the lowest prices in the city.
